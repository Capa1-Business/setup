Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how CaPa's Writing house collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is entirely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
